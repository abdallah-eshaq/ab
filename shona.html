Kutsigira

Health Insurance

Gore rapera vadzidzi vekune dzimwe nyika kuYunivhesiti yeManitoba vakarwadziwa zvikuru nekubviswa kwehuwandu hwehutano hwakabva kune hurumende yeManitoba. Ndicho chimiro changu kuti tinofanira kukurudzira yunivhesiti nekuwana mhinduro inogadzirisa dambudziko iri. Semumiriri weVadzidzi veMarudzi, ini ndinoda kuzvipira zvakakwana kuve nechokwadi chekuti vadzidzi vekune dzimwe nyika vanomiririra zvakakodzera mumangwanani ekukurukurirana kwehutano hutsva hwekutarisira hutano.

Nzira yepamusoro yekunyora kushandiswa

Ikozvino purogiramu yekunyora yekodhi inoratidza chikamu chisina simba apo vadzidzi vanyoresa padzidzo pakati pechikoro chekudzorera-chenguva chinopera uye nguva-kuwedzera nguva yakatarwa ichaongororwa kubhadhara zvakakwana kwekambani iyo, kunyange apo kubhabhatidzwa kunongogara kwemaawa mashomanana. Ndicho chinangwa changu kuti ndikumbire yunivhesiti yekuendesa kuunza sarudzo yeiyo nyaya iyo vadzidzi vemarudzi ose vanotakura mari yakawandisa uye isina kukodzera

Kudzokorora kwegore ra2016 GPA & VW Kuvandudzwa Kwemutemo

Zuva ra2016 rakaona kupera kwekudzoka kuYunivhesiti GPA uye Voluntary Yokubvisa Purogiramu pasina kukodzera kwakakwana kubva kumudzidzi wevadzidzi panguva yematongerwo enyika. Ndicho chimiro changu kuti mitemo iyi inokuvadza mudzidzi ruzivo paYunivhesiti yeManitoba uye semumiriri weVadzidzi veMarudzi ose, ini ndinotarisira kushanda mumakambani nemakambani emudzidzi emakambani ekubvunzurudza yunivhesiti yekudzosa kuchinja mararamiro aya uye kuvadzorerazve kuburikidza nekuvandudzwa kwenyika yepasi rose yepasidduate ruzivo rwepano pano paYunivhesiti yeManitoba

Kukurukurirana

Ita bhi-vhiki nevhiki nhau hurukuro hurukuro

Zvinhu zvakawanda zvinochinja nokukurumidza uye zvinowanzokonzera hupenyu hwevadzidzi vemunyika yose pano paYunivhesiti yeManitoba, kubva pakushandura kuenda kuhutungamiri hwematongerwo enyika kusvika kune zviitiko zvinokosha kubva kumusha. UMSU anozvimirira uye anoshandisa rayo redhiyo uye tinogona kushandisa zvivako izvo UMFM inopa kuti zvinyorwe uye kuparadzira hafu yeawa yehurukuro yemashoko ekuratidza iyo inopfupisa nyaya dzose dzinokosha kupfuura dzose dzinotaura nezvevadzidzi vemarudzi ose. Izvi zvinoita kuti vadzidzi vadzidziswe kuburikidza ne nyore nyore kugadzira uye kufanirwa kwema media.

Zvimwe zvakanyatsozivikanwa zvema email uye nekuenderera mberi kwehuwandu hwemagariro evanhu

Emails anochengetedza vadzidzi vekune dzimwe nyika kuti vazive nezvezviitiko zvinokosha zvinowanzoitika pamusasa ndiyo nzira inokosha uye inokosha yekukurukurirana iyo isu tisingagoni kutora zvishoma. Sevamiriri vevadzidzi vemarudzi ose, ini ndinoda kuva nehana iri nani nekukurukurirana kwema email nevadzidzi kupfuura kare uyewo kuve nechokwadi chekuti mitambo yedu yehupfumi inoramba yakasvike uye

Kubatana

Chirongwa cheMSU Entrepreneurship Grant Competition

Mudzidzi anobata chikamu chikamu chinokosha chehupenyu hweyunivhesiti. Sevamiriri vevadzidzi vemarudzi ose, ini ndinoda kutanga mutambo webhizimisi yekudyisirwa kwebhizimisi apo vashandi vekuchechedza pasi, kunyanya vadzidzi vekune dzimwe nyika vanobvumirana nevana veCanada, vanogona kusvika kune dzimwe nharaunda nematare uye varipo vanokwikwidzana uye mazano emabhizimisi matsva pamwe nechinangwa chekuwana mari yekubhadhara mari inoshandiswa pakutanga pfungwa yekukunda yebhizimisi.

Kubatana pamwe nemayunivhesiti uye pakati pemapoka evadzidzi

Anobatanidzwa uye akabatanidza mudzidzi ruzivo rwakakosha pakubudirira kwevadzidzi paYunivhesiti yeManitoba, zvikurukuru vadzidzi vemarudzi ose. Ndizvo izvi mupfungwa ndinoda kukurudzira kuwirirana kwebato kubva kumapoka evadzidzi uye kuita kuti zviitiko zvekugarisana zvinoshandiswa kupfumisa shanduko yematongerwo enyika tinoona zuva rega rega yunivhesiti, pamwe nekuwedzera kubatana pakati peSUU nemahofisi eunivhesiti iyo inopa mazano anokosha kune dzimwe nyika vadzidzi 'vakaita semasangano.

Kuisa pachena

Vhura mutemo wekutaura

Semumiriri wevadzidzi vemarudzi ose, ndinoda kutanga chirongwa chekutendera vadzidzi vekune dzimwe nyika kuti vatsvake zano nemumiririri wevadzidzi vemarudzi ose pamusoro pemibvunzo nematambudziko ane chokuita nehusiku nemasikati nyaya dzine chokuita neMUSU zvakananga kuburikidza ne email yepamutemo; mushure mezvose, ndinoda mumiririri wenyu kubva mukati meMUU uye munhu ari munzvimbo ino anofanira kunge asina kusvika.

UMSU Mari

Vadzidzi vekune dzimwe nyika vanobhadhara mari yakawanda kuti vakwanise kudzidza pane ino yunivhesiti, izvi zvakawanda zviri pachena. Ndicho chinzvimbo changu chakapiwa mari yatinoshandisa sevadzidzi vemarudzi ose, zvinokosha kuti UMSU ive pachena uye inouya pamusoro pokuti inoshandisa sei mari iyo inomutsa kubva pamari inotarisirwa iyo inobhadharwa pane zvose zviduku

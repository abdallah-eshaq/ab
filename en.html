Policy Guide Vote March 6 - 8

Advocacy Communication Engagement Transparency

Advocacy

Health Insurance

Last year the international students at the University of Manitoba were dealt a major blow by being stripped of coverage of comprehensive & affordable healthcare from the Manitoba government. It is my position that we must lobby the university administration and find a viable solution to this problem. As International Students’ representative, I intend to fully commit to making sure international students are fairly represented in future negotiations for a new healthcare deal.

Better course registration structure

The current course registration schedule exhibits a weak point where students who register for a course between the refundable course-drop deadline and the course-add deadline will be assessed full billing for that course, even when the registration only lasts a for a few hours. It is my intent to lobby the university administration to implement a solution to this issue to which international students bear a disproportionate and unfair cost.

Repeal of 2016 GPA & VW Policy Restructure

Summer 2016 saw the passage of revisions to the University GPA and Voluntary Withdrawals policy without adequate representation from the student body during senate proceedings. It is my position that these policies are a detriment to the student experience at the University of Manitoba and as International Students’ representative, I intend to work in concert with faculty student councils to lobby the university administration to reverse these changes and revise them for the betterment of the international undergraduate student experience here at the University of Manitoba.

Communication

Establish a bi-weekly news bulletin talk show

Many things change very quickly and often that directly affect the lives of the international students here at the University of Manitoba, ranging from changes to immigration policy to important events from back home. UMSU owns and operates its own radio station and we can use the facilities that UMFM provides to record and disseminate a half-hour news bulletin show that summarises all the most important news that pertains to international students. This keeps students informed through an easy to digest and accessible form of media.

More consistent email notifications & continued social media outreach

Emails that keep international students informed of important events happening on campus are a vital and fundamental method of communication that we cannot take for granted. As international students’ representative, I intend to have better consistency with email communications with students than before as well as make sure our social media channels stay up to date and relevant.

Engagement

Proposal for UMSU Entrepreneurship Grant Competition

Student engagement is a vital part of university life. As international students’ representative, I intend to launch a student entrepreneurship competition where undergraduates, especially international students who partner with Canadians, can reach across faculties and departments and present competitive and innovative business ideas with the end goal of attaining a lump sum payout that will be used in launching the winning business idea.

More collaboration with university offices & between student groups

An engaged and interconnected student experience is vital to the success of students at the University of Manitoba, especially international students. With this in mind I intend to foster more intergroup collaboration from student groups and facilitate social events that serve to enrich the intercultural exchange we see everyday at this university, as well as increase collaboration between USMU and offices at the university that provide vital services to international students’ such as workshops and seminars.

Transparency

Open contact policy

As international students’ representative, I intend to institute a policy allowing for international students to seek counsel with the international students’ representative regarding any questions and concerns pertaining to day to day matters related to UMSU directly via official email; after all, I would YOUR representative from within UMSU and a person in this position should be within reach.

UMSU Finances

International students pay a lot of money to be able to study at this university, this much is self-evident. It is my position that given the costs we incur as international students, it is vital that UMSU be transparent and forthcoming about how it uses the funds that it raises from mandatory fees that are levied on every undergrad, especially international students.
